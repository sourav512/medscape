Welcome to our Ai-ML based project, where we use the power of machine learning to predict diseases based on symptoms.

Our team Rahul, Sourav, Gopal, Vaibhav & Ankit (Mentor) is composed of talented and dedicated individuals with a passion for using technology to improve people's lives. Our project is the result of extensive research and collaboration among students from various disciplines, including computer science, data science, and medicine.

We understand the challenges that come with diagnosing diseases, especially in areas with limited access to healthcare. Our project aims to provide a cost-effective and accessible solution to this problem. By training our machine learning model on a large dataset of symptoms and diseases, we can accurately predict the most likely diagnosis for a patient, giving healthcare providers a powerful tool to improve their diagnosis and treatment planning.

At our core, we believe in the power of technology to change the world for the better, and our project is a testament to this belief. We are dedicated to staying on the cutting edge of machine learning, constantly refining our model to improve accuracy and usability. Our project is just the beginning, and we hope to continue using our skills to make a positive impact in the world.
